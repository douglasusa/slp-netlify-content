SLP-CS-002
Genomics / Direct-to-Consumer Retail

Serialized Kitting Infrastructure Scales a Publicly Traded DTC Genomics Program Without Compromising Sample Integrity

Situation

A publicly traded direct-to-consumer genomics retailer faced mounting pressure to scale its consumer saliva sampling program while maintaining uncompromised DNA/RNA integrity, chain-of-custody compliance, and regulatory defensibility. Existing logistics were fragmented, with no unified system governing kit serialization, participant metadata, or last-mile tracking.

SLP's Approach

SLP engineered a fully serialized, private-labeled kitting solution using superior point-of-collection saliva devices capable of stabilizing microbial nucleic acids at ambient temperature. Every kit was assigned a unique ID within SLP CONNECT™, synchronizing barcode tracking with participant data and downstream LIMS integration. DIM Intelligence optimized package architecture, reducing per-unit freight costs across high-volume fulfillment runs.

SLP-CS-005
Precision Medicine / Biomarker Research

Biomarker Discovery Program Gains Statistical Confidence Through Logistics-Correlated Chain-of-Custody Data

Situation

A precision medicine sponsor conducting lipidomic and glycemic biomarker discovery required precise timing windows between specimen collection and laboratory accessioning. Logistical variability — temperature deviations, undefined transit times, and unlabeled handling events — was introducing biological variance that threatened the statistical reliability of the study's mechanistic endpoints.

SLP's Approach

SLP engineered an ambient-temperature nucleic-acid stabilization kitting system and activated SLP CONNECT™'s continuous temperature monitoring and timestamp logging for every shipment. Biological variance was correlatable to logistic data, enabling the research team to isolate and account for pre-analytical contributions to endpoint variability. Automated compliance audit logs were generated within SLP CONNECT™ for FDA and sponsor review.
