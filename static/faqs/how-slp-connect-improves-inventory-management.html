How does SLP CONNECT™ improve inventory management?

SLP CONNECT™ improves inventory management by providing real‑time visibility into stock levels, usage patterns, and replenishment needs across all locations. This eliminates guesswork and ensures that every site has the materials it needs without over‑ordering or running out.

The platform tracks consumption trends, alerts teams when inventory is low, and centralizes ordering behavior so organizations can plan proactively rather than reacting to shortages. This supports consistent, reliable operations across distributed healthcare, research, and precision‑medicine networks.

By integrating directly with SLP’s kitting and fulfillment operations, CONNECT™ creates a unified ecosystem that ensures accuracy, reduces waste, and improves overall supply‑chain performance.
