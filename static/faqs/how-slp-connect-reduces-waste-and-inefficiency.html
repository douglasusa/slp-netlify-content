How does SLP CONNECT™ reduce waste and inefficiency?

SLP CONNECT™ reduces waste and inefficiency by improving ordering behavior, preventing over‑stocking, and ensuring materials are used before expiration. With real‑time visibility into inventory levels and usage patterns, organizations avoid unnecessary purchases and eliminate the guesswork that leads to waste.

The platform highlights slow‑moving items, flags materials approaching expiration, and centralizes ordering so teams only request what they truly need. This reduces excess inventory, lowers costs, and ensures consistent, reliable operations across distributed healthcare, research, and precision‑medicine networks.

By integrating directly with SLP’s kitting and fulfillment operations, CONNECT™ creates a closed‑loop system where every order, shipment, and usage event is tracked — enabling smarter planning, fewer disruptions, and more efficient program execution.
