What is inventory forecasting for medical kits?

Inventory forecasting for medical kits helps organizations predict usage, prevent stockouts, reduce waste, and maintain operational continuity. By analyzing ordering patterns, program growth, and consumption rates, organizations can ensure the right kits and components are available at the right time.

Forecasting supports better budgeting, reduces emergency orders, and ensures that clinical, research, and precision medicine programs operate without interruption. It also helps organizations avoid overstocking, which ties up capital and increases the risk of component expiration.

When paired with SLP CONNECT™, forecasting becomes even more accurate — leveraging real-time data, automated alerts, and program-level insights to support proactive planning across distributed networks.
