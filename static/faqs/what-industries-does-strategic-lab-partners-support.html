What industries does Strategic Lab Partners support?

Strategic Lab Partners is built for organizations that rely on precise, multi‑component kits and controlled deployment. We primarily support:

  • Healthcare and diagnostics – test kits, collection kits, and clinical support kits.
  • Retail and consumer health – at‑home testing, wellness programs, and health‑adjacent products.
  • Clinical research – study kits, site support kits, and controlled distribution to investigators.
  • Consumer goods and specialty products – curated kits that combine multiple SKUs into a single, branded experience.

Across these industries, SLP provides a consistent backbone: program design, kit engineering, manufacturing, warehousing, and 3PL deployment—supported by digital oversight through SLP CONNECT™.
