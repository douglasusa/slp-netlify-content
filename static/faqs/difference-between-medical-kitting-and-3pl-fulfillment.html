What is the difference between medical kitting and 3PL fulfillment?

Medical kitting focuses on assembling standardized kits, while 3PL fulfillment manages storage, shipping, tracking, and return logistics across healthcare and precision medicine programs.

Kitting ensures that every kit contains the correct components in the correct configuration, supporting consistency, compliance, and downstream workflow performance.

3PL fulfillment handles the operational logistics — inventory management, warehousing, order processing, shipping, and returns — ensuring that kits move efficiently through distributed healthcare, research, and consumer networks.

SLP provides both services under one roof, creating a seamless, end‑to‑end operational ecosystem supported by SLP CONNECT™ for real‑time visibility and control.
