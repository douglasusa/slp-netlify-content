What is inventory kitting and how does it work?

Inventory kitting is the process of grouping multiple components into a single SKU to simplify ordering, fulfillment, and tracking. Instead of managing dozens of individual items, organizations can manage one unified kit that contains everything needed for a specific workflow or collection.

Kits are pre-assembled, labeled, and stored as complete units. This reduces handling time, improves accuracy, and ensures that every order contains the correct components in the correct configuration.

When paired with SLP CONNECT™, inventory kitting becomes even more powerful — enabling real-time visibility, forecasting, and automated replenishment across healthcare, precision medicine, clinical research, and ecommerce programs.
