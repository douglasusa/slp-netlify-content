How does the SLP CONNECT™ ecosystem help with compliance?

SLP CONNECT™ enforces clinic-specific authorizations, tracks every item, and maintains secure audit trails. These controls ensure that only approved users can place orders, access inventory, or initiate workflows, reducing the risk of unauthorized activity.

The platform captures every action — from kit creation to shipment to return logistics — creating a complete, timestamped chain of custody. This documentation supports regulatory requirements, internal audits, and sponsor expectations across healthcare, precision medicine, and clinical research programs.

By centralizing compliance controls within SLP CONNECT™, organizations gain consistent oversight, reduced variability, and the ability to demonstrate operational integrity across all kitting and fulfillment activity.
