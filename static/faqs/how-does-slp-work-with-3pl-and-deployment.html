How does SLP work with 3PL and deployment?

SLP integrates kitting with 3PL deployment so that your program doesn’t stop at the warehouse door. We design kits and logistics together, so storage, picking, packing, and shipping all support the way your program actually runs.

Our 3PL and deployment support can include:

  • Warehousing: storing finished kits and related materials.
  • Inventory management: monitoring stock levels and reorder points.
  • Order fulfillment: shipping to clinics, retail locations, study sites, or consumers.
  • Program rules: applying eligibility, geography, or channel rules to shipments.
  • Visibility: using SLP CONNECT™ to give you insight into orders and inventory.

For healthcare, retail, clinical, and consumer programs, this integrated approach means your kits are not only built correctly—they are deployed in a way that supports growth, control, and reliability.
