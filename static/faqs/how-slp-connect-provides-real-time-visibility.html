How does SLP CONNECT™ provide real-time visibility?

SLP CONNECT™ provides real‑time visibility by tracking inventory, usage, orders, and logistics performance across all locations in one centralized platform. This eliminates blind spots and ensures that every stakeholder has accurate, up‑to‑date information.

The platform continuously updates stock levels, consumption trends, and order status, allowing teams to anticipate needs, prevent shortages, and avoid unnecessary over‑ordering. This level of transparency is essential for distributed healthcare, research, and precision‑medicine programs.

By integrating directly with SLP’s kitting and fulfillment operations, CONNECT™ creates a unified ecosystem where data flows seamlessly from warehouse to end‑user — enabling smarter planning, fewer disruptions, and more predictable operations.
