Does SLP offer Saturday fulfillment?

Why customers ask this: Weekend fulfillment can support time‑sensitive programs, but it also increases operational cost—so customers want clarity on when it makes sense.

SLP does not currently offer Saturday fulfillment as a standard service. Most customers have not seen enough operational or clinical value to justify the additional overtime, staffing, and carrier costs associated with weekend operations.

That said, we are open to implementing Saturday fulfillment when a program’s volume, urgency, and economics support it. If weekend operations are important to your workflow, we will work with you to model demand, evaluate cost, and design a staffing and carrier strategy that aligns with your program’s needs.

Our goal is to ensure that any service level we provide delivers meaningful value without introducing unnecessary cost or operational complexity.

SLP Advantage: You get a data‑driven, transparent assessment of when weekend fulfillment truly benefits your program.
