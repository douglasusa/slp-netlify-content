How does SLP CONNECT™ improve compliance and standardization?

SLP CONNECT™ improves compliance and standardization by ensuring every site follows the same validated workflows, ordering rules, and material configurations. This eliminates variability and ensures that all locations operate according to the same approved processes.

The platform enforces consistent ordering behavior, tracks usage patterns, and ensures that only validated kits and components are requested. This reduces errors, prevents unauthorized substitutions, and supports regulatory and operational compliance across distributed networks.

By integrating directly with SLP’s kitting and fulfillment operations, CONNECT™ creates a closed‑loop system where compliance is built into every step — from ordering to inventory management to logistics performance — ensuring reliable, repeatable, and audit‑ready operations.
