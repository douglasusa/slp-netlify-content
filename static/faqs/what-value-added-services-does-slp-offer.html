What value‑added services does SLP offer?

Why customers ask this: They want to understand how SLP supports their entire program—not just shipping boxes.

SLP provides a wide range of value‑added services designed specifically for healthcare and diagnostic programs. These services go beyond traditional 3PL capabilities and help ensure accuracy, compliance, and operational readiness across your entire workflow.

Our value‑added services include:

  • Kit architecture and design — We help define kit components, labeling, ID strategy, and packaging.
  • Unique Test ID generation and management — System‑level controls ensure accuracy and traceability.
  • Component sourcing and vendor coordination — We manage inbound materials and supplier workflows.
  • Inventory forecasting and planning — We model demand and ensure you always have the right stock.
  • Quality system integration — Every workflow aligns with healthcare‑grade quality controls.
  • Program‑level operational support — We help you scale, plan, and optimize your entire program.

These services allow SLP to function as a true operational partner—not just a warehouse—supporting your program from design through national deployment.

SLP Advantage: A fulfillment partner that supports your entire program lifecycle, not just the shipping step.
