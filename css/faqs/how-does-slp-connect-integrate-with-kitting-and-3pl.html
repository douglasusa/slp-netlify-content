How does SLP CONNECT™ integrate with kitting and 3PL?

SLP CONNECT™ integrates directly with SLP’s kitting and 3PL operations, creating a unified system where orders, inventory, and deployment workflows are connected. This ensures that your digital oversight matches real-world activity.

Integration typically includes:

  • Order flow: orders move from CONNECT™ to fulfillment without manual steps.
  • Inventory sync: kit and component levels update automatically.
  • Deployment rules: geography, eligibility, or program logic applied consistently.
  • Reporting: insights into usage, demand, and performance.

This integration ensures that your program is not only operationally sound but also digitally visible and scalable.
