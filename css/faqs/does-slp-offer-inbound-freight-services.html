Does SLP offer inbound freight services?

Why customers ask this: They want to know whether SLP can manage the full flow of materials—from manufacturers to warehouse to final fulfillment—without requiring multiple vendors.

Yes. SLP can manage inbound freight from your manufacturers to our warehouse as part of a full supply chain management workflow. We coordinate carriers, receiving schedules, documentation, and compliance requirements so your materials arrive ready for storage, kitting, or immediate deployment.

Our inbound freight support includes scheduling, tracking, receiving, inspection, and integration into your inventory system. This ensures that every component is accounted for and ready for use in your program without delays or gaps.

Whether you’re sourcing components domestically or internationally, SLP can help streamline the inbound process and reduce the operational burden on your team.

SLP Advantage: You get a single partner overseeing inbound freight, inventory, kitting, and outbound logistics—simplifying your entire supply chain.
