What are the benefits of SKU consolidation in medical kitting programs?

SKU consolidation reduces complexity, improves forecasting accuracy, lowers storage requirements, and simplifies ordering behavior across distributed healthcare and research networks. By reducing the number of SKUs, organizations eliminate confusion, streamline operations, and make it easier for sites to order the correct kits.

Fewer SKUs also improve forecasting accuracy, since demand is concentrated into fewer product lines. This reduces the risk of stockouts, minimizes waste, and supports more predictable budgeting and replenishment cycles.

SLP helps organizations analyze usage patterns, program requirements, and workflow variations to determine where consolidation is possible — often reducing SKU count while improving clarity, consistency, and scalability across all locations.
