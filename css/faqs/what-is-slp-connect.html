What is SLP CONNECT™?

SLP CONNECT™ is the digital oversight layer that gives clients visibility into their kitting and logistics programs. It centralizes information about orders, inventory, and program activity so you can make decisions with confidence.

SLP CONNECT™ typically provides:

  • Order visibility: status, tracking, and history.
  • Inventory levels: real-time counts of kits and components.
  • Program insights: usage patterns, demand trends, and forecasting.
  • Site or location management: multi-site ordering and permissions.

For healthcare, retail, clinical, and consumer programs, SLP CONNECT™ ensures that you always know what’s happening across your supply chain.
