How does SLP CONNECT™ support program visibility?

SLP CONNECT™ gives you real-time visibility into the operational backbone of your kitting and logistics program. Instead of relying on manual updates or spreadsheets, CONNECT™ centralizes the information you need to make informed decisions.

Visibility features include:

  • Order tracking: see order status, carrier details, and delivery confirmations.
  • Inventory dashboards: real-time kit and component levels across all locations.
  • Usage insights: understand which sites or channels are consuming inventory fastest.
  • Program alerts: notifications for low stock, backorders, or exceptions.

With CONNECT™, you always know what’s happening across your supply chain—without waiting for someone to pull a report.
