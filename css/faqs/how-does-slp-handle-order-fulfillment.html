How does SLP handle order fulfillment?

SLP manages order fulfillment for clinics, retail locations, study sites, and consumers through controlled picking, packing, and shipping workflows. Our fulfillment process is designed to support accuracy, speed, and program rules.

Fulfillment typically includes:

  • Order intake: via API, portal, or program rules.
  • Picking: controlled selection of kits or components.
  • Packing: packaging that protects the product and supports your brand.
  • Shipping: carrier selection based on speed, geography, or cost.
  • Tracking: visibility through SLP CONNECT™.

This ensures that every order is fulfilled correctly and consistently, regardless of destination.
