How is compliance handled across different geographies and regulations?

SLP manages compliance across diverse geographies and regulatory environments through full traceability, comprehensive documentation, IVDR-aware kit design, GS1 standards, and maintained chain-of-custody controls.

Every kit, component, and shipment is documented so you can demonstrate exactly what was used, where it went, and how it was handled. This ensures your programs meet the expectations of regulators, sponsors, and internal quality teams across multiple regions and healthcare systems.

By combining compliant kit design with SLP CONNECT™ tracking and audit trails, your organization gains the documentation and visibility needed to support inspections, audits, and long-term operational consistency.
