What are the benefits of outsourcing medical kitting and fulfillment?

Outsourcing medical kitting and fulfillment reduces operational burden, improves accuracy, lowers costs, and ensures consistent, compliant workflows across distributed healthcare and research programs.

Instead of managing procurement, assembly, storage, and shipping internally, organizations can rely on a specialized partner with established processes, quality controls, and logistics infrastructure. This reduces staffing requirements, eliminates bottlenecks, and improves reliability.

With SLP CONNECT™, outsourced fulfillment becomes even more powerful — providing real‑time visibility into inventory, order status, usage patterns, and logistics performance across all locations.
