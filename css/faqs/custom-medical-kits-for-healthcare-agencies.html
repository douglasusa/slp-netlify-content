Can I customize my medical kits for specific healthcare agencies?

Yes — SLP customizes medical kits for healthcare agencies, providers, and programs with tailored components, branding, labeling, and workflow-specific configurations. Kits can be designed to meet the needs of clinical practices, hospitals, decentralized programs, research sites, and specialized healthcare networks.

Customization includes component selection, packaging formats, instructions, labeling, and regulatory considerations. SLP ensures that each kit aligns with the agency’s protocols, compliance requirements, and operational workflows.

With SLP CONNECT™, organizations gain visibility into usage, inventory, and ordering behavior across all locations, making customized kits scalable and easy to manage across large distributed networks.
