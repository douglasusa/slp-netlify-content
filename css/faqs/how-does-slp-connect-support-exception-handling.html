How does SLP CONNECT™ support exception handling?

SLP CONNECT™ supports exception handling by identifying and tracking operational deviations across orders, inventory, and site activity. This ensures issues are caught early and resolved quickly.

Exception handling features include:

  • Low-stock alerts: notifications when inventory reaches critical levels.
  • Order exceptions: backorders, delays, or address issues.
  • Usage anomalies: unexpected spikes or drops in consumption.
  • Program rule violations: orders outside geography, eligibility, or channel logic.

CONNECT™ helps teams stay ahead of problems, maintain program stability, and protect the end-user experience.
