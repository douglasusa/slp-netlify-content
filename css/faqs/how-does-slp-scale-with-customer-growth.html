How does SLP scale with customer growth?

Why customers ask this: They want to know whether SLP can support their growth from pilot volumes to full-scale national or multi‑site deployment.

SLP scales by expanding ship stations, staffing, and supporting infrastructure as your program grows. Because our processes, systems, and quality controls are standardized, we can increase throughput without sacrificing accuracy, visibility, or compliance.

During onboarding, we model growth scenarios—for example, increasing from 1,300 kits per week to 2,500 kits per week over a 12‑month period. This allows us to align staffing, equipment, inventory strategy, and carrier capacity with your projected demand.

Our goal is to ensure that your program can scale smoothly, without bottlenecks or operational surprises, while maintaining the same level of accuracy and reliability at every stage.

SLP Advantage: You get a partner that plans for growth instead of reacting to it—ensuring your program scales predictably and reliably.
