Why Transaction Readiness Matters

March 2026

Most deals fail long before due diligence begins. Not because the business is weak, but because the documentation, processes, and decision‑making structure are not prepared for the scrutiny of a buyer, lender, or investor.

Transaction readiness changes the outcome. It accelerates timelines, reduces friction, increases valuation, and positions the seller as a credible, prepared counterparty.

Readiness is no longer optional — it is the new competitive advantage.
