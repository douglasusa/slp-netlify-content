How do you protect sample integrity for genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, and metabolomics?

SLP protects sample integrity for genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, and metabolomics through analyte-specific kit design, appropriate ambient or cold-chain configurations, controlled timing, and comprehensive metadata capture through SLP CONNECT™.

Kits are engineered to match the stability requirements of each analyte, including the correct collection devices, preservatives, packaging, and temperature controls needed to maintain downstream assay performance.

By pairing purpose-built kits with SLP CONNECT™ tracking and pre-analytical controls, your programs gain consistent handling, reduced variability, and the documentation required to defend data quality in precision medicine, clinical trials, and advanced research workflows.
