How does medical kitting support regulatory compliance?

Medical kitting supports regulatory compliance by standardizing materials, labeling, documentation, and handling procedures across all sites. This reduces variability, improves protocol adherence, and ensures that every collection follows the same validated process.

Regulators expect consistency, traceability, and proper documentation. Pre‑assembled kits help meet these expectations by ensuring that every site uses the correct components and follows the same instructions, reducing the risk of deviations or audit findings.

With SLP CONNECT™, sponsors and CROs gain real‑time visibility into kit usage, logistics, and return workflows — enabling proactive oversight and supporting compliance across multi‑site and decentralized studies.
