What data does SLP CONNECT™ provide?

SLP CONNECT™ provides the operational data needed to run a scalable, predictable kitting and logistics program. It consolidates information from warehousing, fulfillment, and deployment into a single digital view.

CONNECT™ typically provides:

  • Order data: status, tracking, history, and exceptions.
  • Inventory data: real-time kit and component levels.
  • Usage data: consumption patterns by site, region, or channel.
  • Program performance: trends, forecasts, and operational insights.

This data helps teams plan better, reduce risk, and maintain control over program performance.
