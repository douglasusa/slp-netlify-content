How does SLP CONNECT™ improve visibility?

SLP CONNECT™ improves visibility by giving you real-time access to the operational data behind your kitting and logistics program. Instead of relying on manual updates or spreadsheets, you can see exactly what’s happening across your supply chain.

Visibility features include:

  • Order tracking: status, carrier, and delivery confirmation.
  • Inventory dashboards: kit and component levels updated in real time.
  • Usage insights: which sites or channels are consuming inventory fastest.
  • Program alerts: low stock, backorders, or exceptions.

This level of visibility helps teams plan better, reduce risk, and maintain control over program performance.
