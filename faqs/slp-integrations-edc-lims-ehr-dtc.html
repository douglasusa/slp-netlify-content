Can Strategic Lab Partners (SLP) integrate with our EDC/LIMS/EHR or DTC tech stack?

YES — SLP integrates with your EDC, LIMS, EHR, or DTC technology stack through SLP CONNECT™ APIs and data exports, supported by role-based access controls and secure data handling.

This allows your clinical, research, or direct-to-consumer workflows to stay within your existing systems while SLP CONNECT™ manages kitting, fulfillment, tracking, and pre-analytical controls behind the scenes.

By connecting SLP CONNECT™ to your digital ecosystem, you gain consistent identifiers, cleaner data capture, and end-to-end visibility from kit creation through collection, logistics, and lab intake. This reduces manual reconciliation, minimizes errors, and supports audit-ready documentation across your programs.
