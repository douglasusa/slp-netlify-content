How does SLP support decentralized and at-home collections?

SLP supports decentralized and at-home collections by providing standardized specimen-collection kits combined with SLP CONNECT™ onboarding, real-time tracking, and clear user instructions to ensure simple, reliable, and compliant at-home sample collection.

Participants receive kits that are easy to understand and use, while your team gains visibility into kit activation, shipping, and return logistics. This reduces friction, improves compliance, and ensures consistent handling across geographically distributed populations.

By unifying physical kits with digital workflows, SLP helps decentralized trials, remote monitoring programs, and direct-to-consumer offerings maintain consistency, reduce variability, and protect data quality across all collection environments.
