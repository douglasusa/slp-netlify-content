How does SLP CONNECT™ Transformation improve customer experience and NPS?

SLP CONNECT™ Transformation improves customer experience and NPS by strengthening the operational factors that drive satisfaction — accuracy, speed, communication, and consistency — across kitting, fulfillment, and logistics workflows.

The platform reduces errors, eliminates manual reconciliation, and provides real-time visibility into order status, inventory levels, and shipment tracking. This transparency improves trust and reduces customer friction.

By standardizing workflows and enforcing best practices, SLP CONNECT™ helps organizations deliver a more predictable, reliable experience — which directly improves Net Promoter Score (NPS) and long-term customer loyalty.
