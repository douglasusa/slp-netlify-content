Can we edit or cancel orders in real time?

Why customers ask this: They want to avoid wasted shipments, incorrect addresses, and unnecessary costs when patient or site details change.

Yes. Orders can be edited or cancelled in real time as long as they have not yet been released to fulfillment. This includes address corrections, shipment holds, and full cancellations. These changes can be made through agreed-upon workflows or API integrations, ensuring that only valid, up‑to‑date orders move into picking and packing.

Once an order passes a defined release point—such as being picked, packed, or handed to the carrier—changes may require a different process. During onboarding, we work with you to define clear cut‑offs and exception handling rules so your team always knows what is possible at each stage.

SLP Advantage: Real‑time control reduces waste, improves accuracy, and protects the customer experience across your entire program.
