How does SLP support multi-site programs?

SLP supports multi-site programs by giving each location controlled access to ordering, inventory, and program resources. This ensures consistency across clinics, stores, study sites, or partner locations.

Multi-site support includes:

  • Role-based ordering: permissions for who can order and what they can request.
  • Inventory allocation: ensuring each site receives the right quantities.
  • Usage tracking: monitoring consumption by site or region.
  • Program rules: geography, eligibility, or channel-specific logic.

With SLP CONNECT™, multi-site programs gain visibility and control without adding administrative burden.
