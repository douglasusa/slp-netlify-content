How does SLP handle multi‑item orders?

Why customers ask this: They want to ensure that when multiple kits ship together, each kit still maintains its own identity, traceability, and return workflow.

When an order includes multiple test kits, SLP ships them together in a single outbound shipment while preserving full traceability for each individual kit. Every kit retains its own unique Test ID, SKU, lot, expiration, and return label—even when they are packaged in the same box.

Our system tracks the outbound shipment as a whole and each kit within it as a separate, traceable unit. This ensures that every kit can be followed through its complete lifecycle: receipt into inventory, outbound shipment, delivery to the customer, and return to the processing lab.

This approach gives you the efficiency of consolidated shipping with the accuracy and compliance of individual kit‑level tracking.

SLP Advantage: Shipment‑level efficiency with kit‑level traceability—every kit stays unique, visible, and fully documented.
