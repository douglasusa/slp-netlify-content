What makes SLP different from other suppliers?

Most “kit suppliers” focus on components and packaging. Strategic Lab Partners goes further—we design, build, and operate the entire kitting and deployment ecosystem around your program.

Three things set SLP apart:

  1. End‑to‑end ownership: We connect program design, kit engineering, manufacturing, warehousing, and 3PL deployment into a single, accountable workflow.
  2. Domain depth: We understand the realities of healthcare, retail, clinical research, and consumer goods—so kits are designed for real‑world use, not just for a spec sheet.
  3. Digital visibility with SLP CONNECT™: Our digital layer gives you oversight into orders, inventory, and program performance instead of leaving you blind once kits leave the dock.

The result is a partner that doesn’t just ship boxes—we help you build a reliable, scalable, and defensible program that reflects your brand and protects your reputation.
