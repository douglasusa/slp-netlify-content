How does SLP CONNECT™ improve ordering behavior?

SLP CONNECT™ improves ordering behavior by centralizing requests, reducing guesswork, and ensuring teams order the right materials at the right time. Instead of relying on manual tracking or inconsistent local processes, organizations use a single platform that standardizes how orders are placed and approved.

The platform shows real‑time inventory levels, usage trends, and replenishment needs, helping teams avoid over‑ordering, stockouts, or emergency shipments. This leads to more predictable operations, lower costs, and better alignment across distributed healthcare, research, and precision‑medicine networks.

By integrating directly with SLP’s kitting and fulfillment operations, CONNECT™ ensures that every order is accurate, timely, and aligned with validated program requirements.
