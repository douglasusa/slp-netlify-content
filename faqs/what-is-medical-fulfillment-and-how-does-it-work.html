What is medical fulfillment and how does it work?

Medical fulfillment includes inventory management, warehousing, order processing, shipping, tracking, and returns for healthcare and precision‑medicine programs. It ensures that kits, supplies, and materials move efficiently through distributed clinical, research, and consumer networks.

A medical fulfillment partner manages storage, picks and packs orders, ships materials to the correct locations, and handles returns or sample logistics. This reduces operational burden for healthcare organizations, labs, and research teams.

With SLP CONNECT™, organizations gain real‑time visibility into inventory, usage, order status, and logistics performance — enabling proactive planning, fewer delays, and more predictable operations across all sites.
