Why are standardized kits important in clinical trials?

Standardized kits ensure consistency across all sites, reduce protocol deviations, improve sample quality, and support regulatory compliance in clinical trials and research programs. When every site uses the same materials, instructions, and labeling, variability decreases and data integrity improves.

Standardization also reduces the risk of missing components, incorrect materials, or inconsistent handling — all of which can compromise study outcomes or trigger costly delays.

With SLP CONNECT™, sponsors and CROs gain real‑time visibility into kit usage, inventory, shipping, and return logistics, enabling proactive planning and smoother execution across multi‑site and decentralized trials.
