What does Strategic Lab Partners do?

Strategic Lab Partners (SLP) designs, builds, and manages turnkey kitting and logistics programs for organizations that need precise, repeatable, and compliant product deployment. We sit at the intersection of product design, packaging engineering, and 3PL execution, so your kits don’t just look right on paper—they arrive ready for use in the real world.

SLP supports healthcare, retail, clinical studies, and consumer goods brands with end‑to‑end solutions that include:

  • Program design: translating your business, clinical, or retail objectives into a scalable kitting and deployment model.
  • Kit engineering: component selection, packaging design, labeling, and instructions that work in the field.
  • Manufacturing & assembly: building kits at scale with quality controls and documentation.
  • 3PL deployment: warehousing, inventory management, and distribution to clinics, stores, study sites, or end consumers.
  • Digital oversight with SLP CONNECT™: visibility into orders, inventory, and program performance.

In short, SLP turns complex product and kit concepts into operational reality—so your teams, sites, and customers receive exactly what they need, when they need it, in a format they can trust.
