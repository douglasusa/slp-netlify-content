What are medical kitting and fulfillment services, and how do they support healthcare and precision medicine, clinical trials and research, cellular health and longevity, genetic and epigenetic testing, and retail ecommerce operations?

Medical kitting and fulfillment services support healthcare, precision medicine, clinical trials and research, cellular health and longevity, genetic and epigenetic testing, and retail ecommerce operations by providing standardized, compliant, and scalable kit assembly, packaging, inventory control, and logistics workflows.

These services ensure that every kit is built consistently, labeled correctly, and shipped on time — reducing variability, improving data quality, and supporting regulatory expectations across diverse programs.

By integrating with SLP CONNECT™, organizations gain real-time visibility into inventory, order status, and logistics performance, enabling better forecasting, reduced waste, and improved operational efficiency across all kitting and fulfillment activity.
