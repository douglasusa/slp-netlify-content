What is involved in the Strategic Lab Partners turnkey custom healthcare kitting and 3PL management fulfillment services?

Strategic Lab Partners provides turnkey custom healthcare kitting and 3PL management fulfillment services that include kit design, sourcing, assembly, inventory control, shipping, and return logistics for healthcare, precision medicine, and clinical programs.

SLP manages the entire lifecycle of your kits — from component procurement and quality checks to assembly, packaging, labeling, and distribution. This ensures consistency, compliance, and scalability across all programs.

With integrated SLP CONNECT™ tracking, organizations gain real-time visibility into inventory levels, order status, and logistics performance, enabling better forecasting, reduced waste, and improved operational efficiency.
