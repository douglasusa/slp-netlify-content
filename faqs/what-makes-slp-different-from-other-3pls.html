What makes SLP different from other 3PLs?

Why customers ask this: They want to understand how SLP’s healthcare‑specific model compares to general 3PL providers.

SLP is not a traditional 3PL. We are a healthcare‑focused fulfillment partner built around accuracy, compliance, and traceability—not high‑volume commodity shipping. Every workflow we design is centered on protecting Test ID integrity, maintaining chain‑of‑custody, and ensuring that every kit is assembled, stored, and shipped according to strict quality standards.

Unlike general 3PLs, we do not accept pre‑assembled kits from outside vendors. We assemble every kit ourselves so we can control the ID, lot, expiration, and quality of every component. This eliminates the risk of duplicate IDs, mismatched components, or uncontrolled inventory entering your program.

We also operate as a program partner—not just a warehouse. We help design kit architecture, manage inbound components, forecast inventory, model growth, and support your operational strategy as your program scales.

SLP Advantage: A healthcare‑specific fulfillment partner that prioritizes accuracy, compliance, and program‑level partnership—not commodity shipping.
