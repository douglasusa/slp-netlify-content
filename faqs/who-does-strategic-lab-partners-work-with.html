Who does Strategic Lab Partners work with?

Strategic Lab Partners works with organizations that need reliable, scalable, and compliant kitting and logistics support. Our clients typically operate in:

  • Healthcare and diagnostics: labs, diagnostic companies, and health systems that rely on accurate, ready‑to‑use kits.
  • Retail and consumer health: brands that deliver at‑home testing, wellness, or health‑adjacent products through retail or e‑commerce.
  • Clinical research: sponsors, CROs, and site networks that need consistent study kits and controlled distribution.
  • Consumer goods and specialty products: companies that require curated, multi‑component kits delivered to customers or partners.

We are a fit for teams that value precision, repeatability, and visibility—and who want a partner that can bridge the gap between product strategy, kit design, and 3PL execution.

If your organization is scaling a program, launching a new kit, or struggling with fragmented logistics, SLP is designed to operate as an extension of your internal team.
